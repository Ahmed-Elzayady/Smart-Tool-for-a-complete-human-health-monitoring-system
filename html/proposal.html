what is the problem

The problem we suffer from under the conditions of Corona, the person must take distances between him and others to avoid infection and because of the problems of pressure on hospitals, so we designed this project to help Egyptian and military hospitals throughout dear Egypt

The benefit of the project

By using this device, we can reduce the pressure on hospitals from overcrowding, as this device can be placed in the streets and squares and be available for use by anyone, especially in these difficult circumstances.
