Why do We consider this project?

We chose this project because of its great benefit and service to the community and helping patients to check on their health Efficiently and accurately. and To provide an easy and quick way to know the vital processes of the human body, such as blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, etc., and to check on health in general. Usage of this project for community/market/and user Medical

Medical devices are an important means for both the prevention and treatment of diseases in a safe and effective manner at the same time, as achieving these goals requires good management and use by the user of these devices, and thus ensures the improvement of the course of the health medical process and its quality in line with the goals of patients to reach treatment Effective, efficiently and comfortably , And also to medical devices and equipment includes major roles in providing optimal care to patients, which will be mentioned here: Detection and early diagnosis of the problem in its early stages, which helps doctors provide the best possible care. Accurate and detailed results of the diseased condition as soon as possible. Examination and measurement of patients' vital signs. Careful follow up of each diseased case, and identification of the most important possibilities available to reduce pathological dimensions.Providing high resolution images of the surgical site, which helps the medical staff to perform surgeries with ease. Reducing medical errors and complications related to the implementation of complex medical operations, as robots and precision devices work to reduce negative effects. Treating life threatening conditions such as irregular heartbeat. Reducing the risk of infection by minimizing and minimizing surgical intervention. Healing and recovering quickly, which reduces the length of stay in the hospital. Improving the overall quality of the medical system and healthcare providers In short, it is a tool for measuring the temperature of the human body, blood pressure, the number of heart beats, and the percentage of oxygen in the blood for all groups and ages, visually and audibly.
